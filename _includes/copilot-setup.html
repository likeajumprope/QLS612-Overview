Setting up GitHub Copilot in VSCode

In any VSCode window, click the Copilot icon near the bottom right corner of the bottom status bar and select "Use AI Features", then follow the prompts to sign in using GitHub.

This will give you access to the Copilot Free plan in VSCode.

To open the Copilot chat, go to View > Chat.

Reference: https://code.visualstudio.com/docs/copilot/setup
